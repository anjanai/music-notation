Bandish in Bhairavi in Teentaal 

धन धन भाग तेरो जसोदा माँ 
खेलत अंगना कुंवर कन्हाई 
लाल तेरो अन्तर्यामी 
ज्ञानी ध्यानी जाको भेद पायो नाही 

नाचे गाये गोपी ग्वाले नन्द के द्वारे 
हरख हरख सब देत बधाई 
बाल गोपाल को देख्यो 
मन हुलसाये तन पुलकाये बरनि न जाए 

    
dhana dhana bhāga tero jasodā māṁ 
khelata aṃganā kuṃvara kanhāī 
lāla tero antaryāmī 
jñānī dhyānī jāko bheda pāyo nāhī 

nāce gāye gopī gvāle nanda ke dvāre 
harakha hārakha saba deta badhāī 
bāla gopāla ko dekhyo 
mana hulasāye tana pulakāye barani na jāe
Here's a rough translation: 
      Blessed are you, Mother Yashoda
      You have Krishna playing in your courtyard
      Your son is omniscient
      The wise men and thinkers haven't been able to decipher this secret.

      The cowherds are singing and dancing at Nand's door.
      Delighted, they all congratulate him.
      Having seen baby Krishna,
      their hearts rejoice, their bodies enraptured, it's beyond description!
