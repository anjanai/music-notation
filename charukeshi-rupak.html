Bandish in Charukeshi in Rupak

The bandish words are what the gopis say to Uddhav when he visits Brindavan at Krishna's urging (read more about that here). 

      काहे बिसरायो कान्हा 
      संदेसवा लेइ जा 
      गोपियन सो गोविन्द को, ऊधो 
      दरस बिना तरसे जिया बरसे नैना

      जब से गयो मदन मोहन मधुबन सो 
      तरप तरप बीतत उमर 
      हरि सो कहियो, ऊधो 
      बेगि आवो बेगि आवो बेगि आवो
Here's a rough translation: 
      Why did Kanha forget us?
      Take this message from the Gopis to Govind, Oodho (Uddhav)!
      Without seeing him, our hearts yearn (for him), and our eyes shed tears.

      Ever since Madan Mohan left Madhuban,
      Our lives are spent in agony
      Go tell Hari, Oodho...
      Come quickly, come quickly, come quickly!
