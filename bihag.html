Bandish by Pt. Vinayakrao Kale

Hear this bandish being sung 
    बस रही तोरी छब सावर 
    मेरो मन तन मन छावर कियो 
    बिनती करत हूँ हे दयाल कीजे दया
    कुञ्ज कुञ्ज बन में छिप रहे 
    ढूँढत सब ग्वाल बाल 
    चिंता मगन नन्द की रनिया
    
      bas rahi tori chhaba saavara
      mero man tan chhavar kiyo
      binati karat hoon he dayala keeje daya
      kunj kunj ban mein chhip rahe
      dhoondhat sab gvaala baala 
      chinta magan nand ki raniya
Translation: 
    Your image resides in my heart, O dark-skinned one (Krishna)!
    I give myself to you mind, body and soul
    I am requesting you O kind one, have mercy
    You are hiding in the groves in the forest
    All the cowherd boys are searching
    Nand's queen (Krishna's mother) is lost in worries
