Raag Yaman: Shree Guru Sharana

This is the doha at the beginning of the Hanuman Chalisa penned by Tulsidas. The words and their translation are from the wikipedia page on Hanuman Chalisa .

श्री गुरु चरन सरोज रज, निज मनु मुकुर सुधारि
बरनऊं रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायकु फल चारि॥ 		shrī guru charan saroj ruj, nij man mukuru sudhari।
baranau raghuber bimal jasu, jo dayaku phal chari॥

Translation: Cleansing the mirror in the form of my mind with the pollen of the lotus-feet of the Guru, I describe the unblemished glory of Rama, which bestows the four fruits: Dharma (righteousness, moral values), Artha (prosperity, economic values), Kama (pleasure, love, psychological values) and Moksha (liberation, spiritual values)
