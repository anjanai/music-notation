Bandish by Pt. Vinayakrao Kale

Hear this bandish being sung 
	म्हारे डेरे आवो जी महाराजा
	कैसी बिताई दिन रैना
	सावन बीतो फागुन बीतो 
	आई बसंत रुत की बहरिया
    
       mhārē ḍērē āvō jī mahārājā
       kaisī bitāī dina rainā
       sāvana bītō phāguna bītō
       āī basaṃta ruta kī bahariyā
    
      Please come to my abode, Lord!
      How did you spend the day and night?
      Saavan (the fifth month of the Hindu calendar) has come and gone,
      Phagun (the twelfth month of the Hindu calendar) has gone.
      The spring season (the beginning of the Hindu calendar) has come.

Roopak Taal
